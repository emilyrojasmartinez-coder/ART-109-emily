Project 1

I tried to create a couple of brushes that create variety of brush shapes.
To use it, press on the keys 1 thru 7, then click and drag the mouse.
Brush 1 - Rainbow dots with a thin outline
Brush 2 - Cyan Triangle
Brush 3 - Pink Pen
Brush 4 - Circle outline
Brush 5 - Eraser
Brush 6 - Explosion of circles
Brush 7 - Big pink circles
X - to clear the canvas
D - to save the canvas
