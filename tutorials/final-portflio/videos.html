Projection Mapping

Using TouchDesigner to create a projection mapping installation. The installation uses real-time video processing to create an immersive experience. This piece is dedicated to my family and my upbringing. It is a Work in Progress but hope to complete it soon.

Abby's Quinceañera Movie

Partnering up with a Collegue I was able to create a short story film of my sister going missing and my family trying to find her. The film was shot by myself and other family memebers and my friend help me edit it. My role here was Creative Director and Video Director. The film was shot on a DSLR camera and an Iphone camera.
