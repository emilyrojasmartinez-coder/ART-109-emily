Videos

P5.JS Visualization

Using P5 JS I was able to combine three different algorithms to create a visualization. I used the song Supenova Love by Ive to time each scene for my video! Struggles with this project included managing the complexity of integrating multiple algorithms and ensuring smooth performance. Total time spent: 14 hours.

Projection Mapping

Using TouchDesigner to create a projection mapping installation. The installation uses real-time video processing to create an immersive experience. This piece is dedicated to my family and my upbringing. It is a Work in Progress but hope to complete it soon.

Abby's Quinceañera Movie

Partnering up with a Collegue I was able to create a short story film of my sister going missing and my family trying to find her. The film was shot by myself and other family memebers and my friend help me edit it.

My role here was Creative Director and Video Director. The film was shot on a DSLR camera and an Iphone camera. We spent 1 week getting the footage and editing it.

Lyric Video

Using Adobe Premiere Pro to create a lyric video for a song. Always by Daniel Ceasar; the video takes place in the city of San Francisco. Where three girls are remincing on there past life. The video was shot on a DJI Osmo Pocket3 and took 6 hours to complete the editing proccess.

Quince Vlog
